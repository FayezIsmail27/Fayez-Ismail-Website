Project

Lunar Rover

In collaboration with the Canadian Space Agency (CSA), I led a 12-week project with my team to develop a rover capable of navigating the lunar surface. Utilized various skills, including electrical component integration, 3D modeling with SolidWorks, and designing traction mechanisms. Additionally, I transformed 3D models into functional components using advanced 3D printing and precision cutting techniques in the workshop. We documented each step in a weekly report to ensure thorough communication and project tracking with our client.

Automated Fluid-Powder Dispenser

Led the team in a 12-week project to model and build an Automated Fluid Dispenser that accurately dispenses precise amounts of fluid and powder, crucial for the consistent creation of medications. Applied diverse skill sets in 3D modeling (SolidWorks), Arduino programming (C++), workshop fabrication, and electrical engineering. Throughout the project, I emphasized detailed documentation and maintained transparent communication with stakeholders to ensure alignment with project goals.
